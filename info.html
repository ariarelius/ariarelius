Ari Árelíus is a musician from Reykjavík Iceland. He will be releasing his first ep in 2018 titled Emperor Nothing. The first single of the forthcoming album Won’t Ever Stop has received the praise of critics and been played generously on the national radio. His music has been described as alternative pop and has a vast array of influences, Jeff Buckley, Marvin Gaye and Air to name a few.
