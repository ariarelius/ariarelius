Ari Árelíus is a musician from Reykjavík Iceland. Made in France, born in Sweden and raised in various places on a rocky little island in the north. His music has been described as alternative pop rock or indie jazz and has a vast array of influences, King Krule, Marvin Gaye and Air to name a few. He released his first EP in August 2018 titled Emperor Nothing.
